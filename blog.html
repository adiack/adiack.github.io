[Release notes](https://laion.ai/blog/large-openclip/) ## Google PhD Fellowship list for 2022 is out Congratulations to all Google PhD Fellowship 2022 recipients. Recipients are provided with funding for research activities as well as optional mentorship. Many recipients this year are from African institutions. [Google PhD Fellowship](https://research.google/outreach/phd-fellowship/) ## COYO-700M - an Image-Text Pair Dataset COYO-700M, a large-scale dataset containing 747M image-text pairs as well as many other meta-attributes has been released. Goal of this dataset is to increase the usability to train various models. It can be used to train popular large-scale foundation models complementary to other similar datasets. [Gitbug Page](https://github.com/kakaobrain/coyo-dataset)
